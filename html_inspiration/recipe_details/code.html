Heirloom Roasted Tomato & Basil Soup

A deep, concentrated flavor profile achieved through slow-roasting seasonal vine tomatoes with whole garlic cloves and fresh thyme.

schedule

Prep Time

15 mins

oven_gen

Cook Time

45 mins

bar_chart

Difficulty

Intermediate

group

Servings

4 Portions

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Vine-ripened tomatoes, halved 1.4 kg
  • Whole garlic bulb, top sliced off 1 unit
  • Extra virgin olive oil 1/4 cup
  • Fresh basil leaves 1 cup
  • Vegetable stock 500 ml
  • Heavy cream (optional) 60 ml

Chef's Tip

"For a smokier profile, roast the tomatoes until the skins are slightly charred."

The Method

01

Roast the Foundation

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the tomatoes on a large parchment-lined baking sheet. Place the garlic bulb in the center. Drizzle generously with olive oil and season with sea salt and cracked pepper.

timer 40 Minutes at 400°F
02

Extract the Essence

Once the tomatoes are soft and slightly caramelized, remove from the oven. Carefully squeeze the roasted garlic cloves out of their skins directly onto the pan. The garlic should be soft and buttery.

03

Blend and Simmer

Transfer the contents of the pan (including the juices) into a high-speed blender. Add fresh basil and vegetable stock. Blend until velvety smooth. Pour into a pot and simmer for 5 minutes to marry the flavors.
